What are the steps?

At AMW Veranda we are happy to offer a tailor-made offer to our customers. Requesting a quote is simple and fast, and our step-by-step plan gives you a clear overview of your veranda in a few minutes. Below we will explain the three steps of requesting a quote.

Roadmap

  • Contact details

  • Materials and products

  • Size information and notes

In the first step we ask you for your contact details. This way we know who we can contact and we can keep you informed of the status of your quote. In the second step you can choose your desired materials and products. In the last step you can enter the dimensions of the place where you want the veranda. In addition, you can add any comments, so that we get an even better picture of your veranda idea.

These steps give us a clear picture of your veranda idea and we can offer you a tailor-made offer. It is useful to request a quote, because this way we can give a realistic price indication and keep you informed of any discounts and offers. Do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or would like more information about our products and services.

Enjoy your garden to the fullest!

AMW Veranda offers unlimited possibilities for the realization of verandas. Thanks to our many years of experience and expertise, we can design a veranda that perfectly matches your home, based on your specific wishes and expectations. Whether you are looking for a veranda in a traditional or modern design, we provide a solution that integrates as much as possible as extra living space for every season. Our verandas are available in aluminum, wood and a combination of both materials, so that we can always meet your preference and style.

Our coordination

At AMW Veranda we take full responsibility for the realization of your veranda. We strive to design your veranda in detail according to your wishes and expectations and to offer you extra living space for every season. You have the freedom to determine the desired degree of finish and we coordinate the installation of air conditioning, heating, electricity and lighting. At AMW Veranda you can rely on our expertise and professional approach to make your veranda project a success.

About us

Our Story

The AMW Veranda is an all-in-one solution for those looking for a veranda or other types of roof. It is a partnership of companies that complement and reinforce each other in the field of knowledge, craftsmanship and expertise. The participating companies have years of experience and guarantee top quality and service, from design to installation.

We strive to provide our customers with the ultimate in outdoor living. We believe that every home deserves to be enriched with a veranda that is not only practical and stylish, but also offers an extension of indoor life.

AMW Veranda,

Wide selection of canopies for every style and wish

With our range of verandas, garden rooms, sheds, slatted roofs and pergola roofs, we offer a wide range of roofs for every wish and taste. In addition, we offer a variety of extensions and options, from wall systems to sun protection, with which you can design your veranda in a unique and practical way. Our employees are qualified and trained in the field, so that they can advise and guide you in an expert manner. You can also be trained with us to become a specialist in installing roofs.

AMW Veranda has been active for more than 14 years and stands for 100% quality and service. We use our own production, which means we can switch quickly and guarantee a fast and efficient execution of your project. In addition, we offer the possibility to come and measure for free, so that we can present a tailor-made design.

About AMW Veranda

AMW Veranda offers elegance and comfort with its high-quality verandas. The company is known for the best prices in the market and a full coordination from realization to finish. Enjoy every season in your garden in style with a veranda from AMW.
